About This Website

The BTA Application tool provides learners and teachers access to review WAEC past questions and set standard examination-style questions for practice. BTA is essential for students preparing for any major academic or promotional examination. This App is subject-specific and adapts to any academic subject or course within the Ghanaian curriculum.

Soon you will be able to download BTA for all subjects in the Ghana Senior High School curriculum and for all tertiary courses!
